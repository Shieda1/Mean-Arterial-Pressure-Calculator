What is the mean arterial pressure calculator?
The mean arterial pressure calculator is a tool that determines the average arterial blood pressure during a single cardiac cycle.
How to calculate pulse pressure?
To calculate pulse pressure you should use the following formula:
pulse pressure = systolic blood pressure - diastolic blood pressure
